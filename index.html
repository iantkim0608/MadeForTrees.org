Welcome to made for trees

Made for Trees is a non-profit organization led by CI students dedicated to change major environmental problems in our local community. Obviously, one of our main goals is to plant trees and spread their importance around the globe. This not only benefits the environment, but also generations to come!

Why do these things?

Today, about 82 million hectares (202 million acres) of forests are burnt by wildfires. This effects the wildlife in the surrounding areas making them extinct or endangered. However, By contributing and being engaged in our service activity we could improve some of the problems.

Just 4 dollars to save the Earth

In our organization, we are planning to plant several trees in local parks to help the environment. We will do this by raising money using fundraisers at large school events or coordinating an event during Community Time, after school, or during weekends, which will be our primary route of preparing the resources for our project. A tree can absorb 48 pounds of C02 and produce 260 pounds of oxygen annually. The Earth will benefit from each one of your 4 dollars

We need your help!

Donations and funds are crucial to the project that we are planning. Please donate to us and work for our fundraisers. In addition, this project is not only the work of us. You can always visit a nearby park or natural environment to help us plant and save the environment. In order for us to succeed, your help will play an essential part!
