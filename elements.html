Aims

What ‘Made for Trees’ Aim to Do:

  • Spreading Awareness about Wildfires

    Made for Trees focuses on wildfire and the animals that get endangered from them.

  • Mitigate Carbon Emission

    Made for Trees tries hard to reduce CO2 emissions as it harms our ecosystem which would pass through future generations

  • Plant Trees

    Made for trees also focus on deforestation around the world which affects climate change, desertification, soil erosion, flooding, and loss of animal habitats.
